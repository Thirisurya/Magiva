Field sales is a vast domain that involves a range of activities like beat plan, outlet visits, order taking, stock audits, contacts and lead management, attendance and activity tracking of sales team members. Using conventional manual methods to document the data from these transactions are time-consuming, inefficient and low on reliability.

This drove to the development of a one-stop technological solution to streamline the entire sales process – the Salesmatic. It has a user-friendly interface, and can be operated from mobile and/or computer devices of choice.
